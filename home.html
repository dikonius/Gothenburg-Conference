BÄTTRE WEBB CONFERNCE

GOTHENBURG

OCTOBER 18-20, 2024

Since the invention of the World Wide Web in 1989, Bättre Webb is a yearly international academic conference on the topic of the future direction of Frontend Development. This conference has been the premier venue to present and discuss progress in research, development, standards, and applications of the topics related to the Web development. The conference assembles scholars, researchers, policymakers and practitioners with one unifying goal: to envision and create the future of the Frontend Development

The 2024 Bättre Webb Conference will offer a high quality program made of 10 unique seminars led by prominent people in IT sphere. Semianrs will include posters, demonstrations, workshops, tutorials, a resource track for the practitioners as well as thought provoking keynote speakers, panels and special tracks.

Bättre Webb is an in-person conference with rich virtual components including live streaming of keynotes, oral presentations, workshops and tutorials, as well as access to pre-recorded videos of talks.
